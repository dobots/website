Caring

"To live independently at home" in our aging population. A phrase, quickly uttered, but what can we really do to help our elderly and ourselves in the future?! DoBots develops the virtual butler, a system around our crownstone technology to aid elderly in communication and daily tasks.

Read more at the page about our virtual butler.

Flextime and flexplace

Where are my colleagues? Flexible working, flex-working, flextime, and flexplace is a new right and a new challenge for modern employees as well as employers. DoBots develops the virtual office manager, a system around our crownstone technology that tells colleagues who is where at what times!

Read more at the page about our virtual office manager.

Hospitality

DoBots develops the virtual host, a system around our crownstone technology to make your stay in hotels, bars, and cruise ships even more pleasant.

Read more at the page about our virtual host.

Home

DoBots makes it possible to bring future trends in which homes becomes smarter to contemporary times. Our crownstones provide the eyes and ears to our homes. This means that our homes can come alive (they can actually start to take care of us, and anticipate our wishes). Not only that, our homes can go on standby. This type of technology is not just for early adoptors, it is perhaps even more for eco-friendly people. Nobody is too lazy to turn on a light, but aren't we at times to turn them all off, when we go to the bathroom for a moment?

Read more at the page about our smart homes.

Cleaning

DoBots offers an autopilot for a cleaning machine. A scrubber/dryer requires a cleaning person to walk behind it. DoBots provides an add-on to a robot, a so-called autopilot, to upgrade a machine to a fully autonomous robot.

Read more at the page about the autopilot for cleaning.
